Climber

• Able to climb next to another team
• Climb in 2 seconds

Elevator

• Cascading elevator
• Can push up to 6.79 feet in 1.5 seconds
• Motion profile control
• Can stack two cubes on the scale in autonomous period
• Can stack 2 cubes high on a losing scale

Cube Handler

• Can pick up cubes in both orientations
• Can launch or drop cubes

Drivetrain

• 6 wheel drive
o 2 Omni Wheels
o 4 Traction Wheels
• Geared for a max. speed of 15 FPS

Infeed

• Grabs and aligns cubes from any position
• Utilizes “PID” position control
